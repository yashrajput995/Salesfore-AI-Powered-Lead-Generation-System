
# Understanding Business Requirements
### Salesforce AI-Powered Lead Generation System

## 1. Overview
Understanding business requirements is a critical step in the development process. It focuses on identifying the goals, challenges, and expectations of stakeholders to ensure the proposed solution aligns with organizational objectives.

For the Salesforce AI-Powered Lead Generation System, this stage aims to understand how businesses currently generate leads, the problems they face, and how AI and Salesforce integration can improve the efficiency of the sales process.

## 2. Business Goals
- Improve the efficiency of lead generation.
- Increase the conversion rate of potential customers.
- Reduce manual effort in identifying and managing leads.
- Provide data-driven insights to support sales decisions.
- Enable sales teams to prioritize high-quality leads.

## 3. Business Challenges
Organizations often face the following challenges in lead management:
- Manual lead identification which consumes time.
- Difficulty in prioritizing high-value leads.
- Lack of insights into customer behavior.
- Inefficient use of CRM data.
- Limited automation in the sales pipeline.

## 4. Key Stakeholders
Sales Manager
- Monitors sales performance
- Reviews lead conversion reports
- Assigns leads to sales representatives

Sales Representatives
- Interact with potential customers
- Follow up on assigned leads
- Update lead status in the system

System Administrator
- Manages system configurations
- Maintains CRM integration
- Ensures system security and access control

Business Analysts
- Analyze business needs
- Translate business requirements into system requirements

## 5. Current Workflow
1. Collect customer data from multiple sources.
2. Manually review and evaluate potential leads.
3. Enter lead information into the CRM system.
4. Assign leads to sales representatives.
5. Follow up with potential customers.

## 6. Proposed Solution
The system will introduce AI-powered lead analysis integrated with Salesforce CRM.

Key improvements include:
- Automated lead data collection
- AI-based lead scoring
- Lead classification (Hot, Warm, Cold)
- Smart lead prioritization
- Real-time dashboards and reports

## 7. User Roles
Sales Manager – Monitor sales performance and view reports  
Sales Representative – Manage and follow up on assigned leads  
System Administrator – Maintain system configuration and security

## 8. Expected Business Outcomes
- Faster lead identification and prioritization
- Improved sales productivity
- Higher lead conversion rates
- Better utilization of CRM data
- Increased business growth and customer engagement

## 9. Conclusion
Understanding business requirements ensures that the Salesforce AI-Powered Lead Generation System addresses real organizational needs. By aligning technology with business objectives, the system helps sales teams make smarter decisions and improve overall performance.
